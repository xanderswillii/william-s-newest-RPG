Hollow Night
Hollow Night is a 2d Metroidvania Game in the undergroung World. Here you will play as "Griffith" a small explorer driven by curiousity
to explore the underground world.

Gameplay
Explore the underground world full of secretes and hidden paths 
Navigate thriugh interconnected levels 
Survive enemies attacks 

Build With
Godot Engine 4.6- GD Script
2D platformer with metroidvania-style level design

Controls
use the Arrow Keys to move left and right
use x to swing the sword to hit enemies and destroy some objects
Jump with Spacebar
